{! Roq seems to treat collections in the opposite order than Jekyll does. With Jekyll, if I'm on a post from July 17 and I click "Next", I would get a post from, say, July 18. With Roq, however, I would get a post from July, say 16. To keep the navigation the same, we'll have to use next/previous with the opposite label. !}
